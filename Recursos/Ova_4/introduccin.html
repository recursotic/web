Introducción

When talking about past, in this particular case: PAST PERFECT and SIMPLE PAST we need to have clear how and why we NARRATE and RELATE PAST EVENTS, as well as how and why we SET EVENTS THAT STARTED AND FINISHED IN THE PAST, BUT THEY CAN BE FURTHER or SPECIFIC in the Past. At the end of surfing this source, we will comprehend and apply the differences and contrasts between PAST PERFECT and SIMPLE PAST.
