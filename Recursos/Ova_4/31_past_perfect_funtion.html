3.1. Past Perfect Funtion

 

Simple Past and Past Perfect work in contrast to show order of events:

Example

By the time  President Suárez set the Military Aviation (31st December 1919), the commercial aviation: SCADTA had already started in Colombia (5th December 1919).

Example

https://www.fac.mil.co/en-el-comando-a%C3%A9reo-de-mantenimiento-hoy-se-entrega-el-%C3%BAltimo-avi%C3%B3n-t-90-calima)

Before the T-90 was the training airplane, the Caudron had been a training equipment for pilots.

Example

But also, it goes back much earlier in past.

When Esufa established in Madrid, there had already been a School of Mechanics in Mariquita.

Notice that by the time gives a point reference in past about the war time

Notice that before gives a point reference in past about a previous plane for training

Notice that when goes back to a previous event related to the creation of the school
