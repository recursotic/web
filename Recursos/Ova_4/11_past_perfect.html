1.1. Past Perfect

 

Relates to events or actions that started before other one in the past. Therefore, it usually has connection or relation with other event in the past. That is to establish a chronological order or go back to time to mention further or previous events in past.

Example

By  1916, President Concha had expressed the need to have an air force.

Notice that by gives a point reference in past about the action or the event
