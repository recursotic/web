Introducción

When talking about events or actions based on time, in this particular case: SIMPLE PAST and PRESENT PERFECT we need to have clear how and why we NARRATE PAST EVENTS, as well as how and why we MENTION EVENTS THAT STARTED AND FINISHED IN THE PAST, BUT THEY ARE STILL or HAVE EFFECTS on Present. That´s is something challenging to understand, but we will comprehend and apply for sure at the end of the exploration of this object the differences and contrasts between SIMPLE PAST and PRESENT PERFECT.
